Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a chronic autoimmune disease that causes rapid skin cell growth. It results in thick, red patches with silvery scales.

Causes

  • Genetic factors
  • Immune system malfunction
  • Environmental triggers

Symptoms

  • Red patches covered with silvery scales
  • Itching and pain
  • Thickened nails
  • Joint pain or stiffness
  • Scalp irritation

Treatment

Psoriasis treatment aims to control symptoms and reduce inflammation. Options include topical corticosteroids, vitamin D analogs, phototherapy, and systemic medications (methotrexate, biologics). Treatment plans will differ based on the severity of the condition.
